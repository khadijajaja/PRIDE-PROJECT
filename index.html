places must visit

prithvi cafe

i had visited in dec 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.



jantar mantar - jaipur

i had visited in feb 2019

It's a wonderful place. Must visit this place. Must haired tour guide so it will be batter to under stand all the things. It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls. It's a historical site famous among tourists. It contains a number of different devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the Indian history. It has a nominal ticket price of ₹15 for Indians but about ₹225-250 for foreigners. Nice place to visit. We had an incredible experience visiting this observatory and seeing the accuracy of the astronomical instruments built around 300 years ago



yiga choeling monastery

i had visited in oct 2019

One of the oldest Monastery in Darjeeling. It is situated in peaceful and beautiful landscape just 15 minutes walk from Ghoom Railway Station. We can see religious God and Goddess paintings believed by Buddhism inside which can give spiritual power to one who prays there with clean heart. From the compound of Monastery on a clear day one can have a scenery of hills all around which is great for nature lovers.

review on the movies

Yeh Jawaani Hai Dewaani
Astonishinh Bollywood movie!

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a beautiful work of art that perfectly depicts the spirit of youth, love, and personal development. The movie expertly weaves together the complex webs of friendship and romance thanks to the mesmerizing performances of Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone. Every scene resonates because to the masterful directing of Ayan Mukerji, who masterfully combines magnificent images and a soul-stirring soundtrack. Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a remarkable cinematic experience that merits its place among the best thanks to the story's genuineness and relatability as well as its heartfelt representation of self-discovery, ambition, and the complexities of relationships. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Top Gun
2 Time watch movie

With thrilling aerial moments, a compelling performance by Tom Cruise, and a catchy '80s music that emphasizes the action-packed plot, Top Gun rises as a classic in the action movie genre. The thrilling flight sequences and the connection between Cruise's Maverick and Iceman, played by Val Kilmer, demonstrate director Tony Scott's skill. While some character development is lacking and the movie leans heavily on its machismo, Top Gun's influence on pop culture and the pure excitement of its dogfighting sequences make it a must-see for action fans. ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Spider Man: across the spider-verse
Thrilling, exciting and will leave you on the edge of your seat

"Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" deserves an enthusiastic five stars for its groundbreaking animation, innovative storytelling, and exhilarating action. This sequel continues to push the boundaries of the superhero genre with its mesmerizing visuals that seamlessly blend various art styles and universes. The film's multiverse concept is expertly executed, introducing audiences to new iterations of Spider-People while deepening the emotional journey of Miles Morales. The dynamic characters, heartfelt relationships, and witty humor are woven into a compelling narrative that explores identity, growth, and the interconnectedness of different realities. With a pulsating soundtrack and a fresh take on the Spider-Man mythos, this film cements its place as a modern animated masterpiece that caters to fans and newcomers alike. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Global Issues

  • Climate change is damaging our homes.

    One of the pressing problems facing the world today is climate change. The Earth's climate is undergoing significant and rapid changes due to human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes. This has led to a rise in global temperatures, resulting in more frequent and severe weather events, melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and disruptions to ecosystems. Climate change poses a serious threat to the environment, human societies, and economies, impacting everything from food and water security to public health and geopolitical stability. Addressing this issue requires global cooperation, sustainable practices, and the development of clean energy solutions to mitigate its potentially catastrophic consequences.

  • Cyber Saftey.

    Cybersecurity and digital threats have become a prominent global issue. As the world becomes increasingly interconnected through technology, the risk of cyberattacks, data breaches, and digital espionage has grown. Malicious actors, including hackers and state-sponsored entities, target individuals, businesses, critical infrastructure, and even governments. These attacks can result in financial losses, compromised personal information, disruption of essential services, and erosion of privacy. Countering this problem necessitates robust cybersecurity measures, international collaboration, and the development of strong digital defenses to safeguard individuals, organizations, and nations from the evolving landscape of cyber threats.

FNAF- into the pit

Into the pit

"Five Nights at Freddy's: Into the Pit" is a gripping horror novella by Scott Cawthon and Elley Cooper that delves into the eerie mysteries of the popular video game franchise. Set in the eerie Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, the story follows Oswald, a teenager who stumbles upon a time-traveling ball pit that leads him to different eras in the pizzeria's twisted history. As Oswald becomes entangled in the sinister events of the past, he must confront the animatronic horrors that haunt the establishment and uncover the truth behind the dark forces at play. The novella masterfully weaves elements of suspense, time travel, and the franchise's signature creepiness, offering a chilling expansion to the Five Nights at Freddy's universe.

From me to you


Kimi ni todoke

"Kimi ni Todoke," also known as "From Me to You," is a heartwarming and beautifully crafted Japanese manga and anime series. The story revolves around Sawako Kuronuma, a shy and misunderstood high school girl often compared to the horror movie character Sadako due to her appearance and quiet demeanor. Her life takes a transformative turn when she befriends Shouta Kazehaya, a popular and outgoing boy who sees beyond her exterior. The series beautifully portrays Sawako's journey of self-discovery and her blossoming friendships, particularly with Kazehaya. Through its gentle and touching narrative, "Kimi ni Todoke" captures the essence of genuine connections, personal growth, and the power of acceptance and kindness.
